"Water Supply Chain Management" is web application. The main Objective of this application is to handle the supply chain management between provider, consumer at one platform.
Project Modules: (Features)
1.  Profile Management (Login/Sign Up):   1) Distributer   2) Customer
2.  Order Management (New Order/My Order)
3. Distribution Management (Distribute)
4.  Commercial Management (COD/Online Pay)
5.  Feedback System
6. Delete Order
